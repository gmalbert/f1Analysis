# Formula 1 Analysis — 6-Month Feature Roadmap

## Month 1: Race Weekend Experience

- **Qualifying predictions** — Model grid position predictions published after FP2 using practice session data.
- **Live timing widget** — Embed the F1 live timing stream URL on a Race Day page.
- **Compound strategy recommender** — Given pit window and track position, show likely optimal strategy.

## Month 2: Driver & Constructor Pages

- **Driver season profile** — Elo trend, MAE per race, qualifying delta vs. teammate, DNF count.
- **Constructor battle tracker** — Points gap between constructors P2–P4; model-implied finishing order.
- **Rookie tracker** — Special section for drivers in their first full season with rookie simulation.

## Month 3: Betting & Edge Tools

- **Race winner probability vs. odds** — Compare model win probability to DraftKings race winner market.
- **Podium probability table** — Top-6 drivers with % chance to finish P1, P2, P3.
- **Driver prop value finder** — Compare model's predicted position against DraftKings "Driver H2H" props.

## Month 4: Historical Analysis

- **Race replay analysis** — For completed races, show model prediction vs. actual result with delta analysis.
- **Circuit characterisation page** — Historical stats per circuit: avg winner, overtaking rate, SC frequency, podium diversity.
- **Season history browser** — Filterable table of all race results from 1950+ via F1DB.

## Month 5: Email & Notifications

- **Pre-race email (Thursday)** — Model predictions and DraftKings odds for the upcoming Grand Prix.
- **Post-race accuracy report** — Auto-generated Monday email with MAE for the weekend's predictions.
- **Qualifying alert** — Push notification when the predicted pole sitter differs from the market favourite.

## Month 6: Advanced Analytics

- **Tyre strategy simulator** — Interactive tool to model alternative pit strategies.
- **Weather probability integration** — FP/Qualifying weather forecasts from Open-Meteo to adjust predictions.
- **Multi-race scenario planner** — "If Hamilton wins 3 of the last 5 races, what are the championship probabilities?"
